1. Login to your Gmail account (regular email at Gmail.google.com)
2. Click in the gear icon at the top right side of the screen
3. Click in "Settings"
4. Click in the "Filters" tab
5. Click in "Create a new filter"
6. In the "To" field please add your own email address (i.e. if you are inside of your email account, me@mydomain.com, you need to put your address me@mydomain.com)
6. Click in "Create filter with this search"
7. Click in "Never send it to Spam"
8. Click in "Create filter"
